What We Do !

We empower millions of rural dairy farmers and deliver high-quality, affordable, and trusted dairy products to every Indian household.

At Amul, our journey goes beyond simply producing dairy products. We are a symbol of the White Revolution in India‚Äîa movement that reshaped the rural economy and brought self-sufficiency to the dairy industry. Our cooperative model connects farmers directly to processing and distribution networks, eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair prices. This not only uplifts the economic conditions of countless farmers but also guarantees the freshest milk reaches your home without compromise. From a glass of morning milk to rich and creamy ice creams, Amul touches every stage of life with products crafted to nourish, energize, and delight. We manufacture a wide range of dairy items such as milk, butter, cheese, paneer, curd, chocolates, beverages, sweets, and more. Every product undergoes rigorous quality testing to ensure it matches global standards while retaining the taste and authenticity that Indians love. But we don't stop there. At the heart of Amul lies innovation. We constantly introduce new products, adapt to changing consumer tastes, and invest in technology to enhance efficiency and sustainability. From solar-powered chilling units in rural collection centers to eco-friendly packaging solutions, we care for the planet just as much as we care for our people. Our network is vast ‚Äî thousands of village societies, hundreds of processing plants, and distribution that spans every corner of the country. Yet, our purpose remains simple: to make high-quality nutrition accessible to every Indian household while uplifting those who produce it. Amul stands for more than milk. It stands for unity, opportunity, growth, and the sweet taste of success shared by all.
